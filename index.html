Manage Pension Account


This contains the primary app function. Which is to keep customers up to date on their Retirement savings account, chat with a customer care agent and find the nearest Aiico branch when the need arises.

Create Reminders and set Goals


A custom strategic planner & daily agender for planning and living a well designed life. Making sure you don't forget or skip your daily goals with personalised p.a reminding you of your set goals for the day, the ones you've done and the reminder for the ones you have not.

Retire in Style


This section of the app is the fun part of the app where users fill what they want their retirement to look like. Like what type of car they want to drive, lifestyle, vacations and so on.


Also, pension remitted monthly, retirement age this will then give an aggregate of how much more they need to remit per month (if they already have an RSA) or how much they need to remit (if they don't).

Personalized Daily Content Digest


Tailored content for app users. This content is based on interest varying from sport, news, arts & culture selected by the users upon signing up for the app.

Retirement Calculator


This is the core function of the app, users are asked to fill in basic informations about themselves like age, pension remitted monthly, retirement age and so on.
